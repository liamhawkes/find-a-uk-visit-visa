Find a UK visitor visa

Standard visitor visa

You can visit the UK on holiday, to see friends and family, take part in sports or creative events, receive medical treatment.

Work: No

Study: Yes (up to 30 days)

Cost: £89

Length of stay: up to 6 months

Marriage Visitor visa

You can apply for a Marriage Visitor visa you want to get married or register a civil partnership in the UK.

Work: No

Study: Yes (up to 30 days)

Cost: £87

Length of stay: up to 6 months

Parent of a Tier 4 child visa

You can apply for a Parent of a Tier 4 child visa if your child attends an independent fee-paying day school in the UK.

Work: No

Study: No

Cost: £496

Length of stay: 6 or 12 months

Permitted Paid Engagement visa

You may be able to get a Permitted Paid Engagement visa if you’ve been invited to the UK as an expert in your profession.

Work: Yes

Study: Yes (up to 30 days)

Cost: £87

Length of stay: 1 month

Short-term study visa

Apply for a Short-term study visa for a short course of study or training in the UK.

Work: No

Study: Yes (up to 30 days)

Cost: £93 (6 months) or £179 (11 months)

Length of stay: 6 or 11 months

Chinese tour group

Chinese citizens can Come to the UK as part of a tour group through the approved destination status agreement (ADS).

Work: No

Study: No

Cost: -

Length of stay: up to 30 days
